About Liver Disease

Liver diseases are often caused by excessive alcohol consumption, fatty liver, and infections like hepatitis. By predicting your risk early, you can take preventative measures.

Alcohol

Excessive alcohol consumption can cause liver inflammation and cirrhosis.

Fatty Liver

A buildup of fat in the liver can lead to serious health issues if untreated.

Hepatitis

Viral infections such as Hepatitis B and C are common causes of liver damage.
